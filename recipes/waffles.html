Waffles Recipe

Description

This waffle recipe is the only one you'll need to make homemade waffles with your waffle iron.
Simple pantry ingredients mix up quickly in this easy batter that can be used right away or stored in the refrigerator
for up to a week.
Serve waffles hot with whipped cream and fresh fruit or with butter and maple syrup for either breakfast, brunch, or a snack.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • sugar
  • spices and seasoning
  • noodles
  • cheese
  • eggs

Steps to Follow

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
